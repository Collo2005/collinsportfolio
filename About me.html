About Me

Collins Muriithi Mwaniki
Tech Enthusiast | AI Advocate | Digital Inclusion Champion

Collins is a passionate tech innovator dedicated to bridging the digital divide in rural Kenya. With a strong background in web development — specializing in Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — he builds user-friendly, impactful digital solutions that empower communities.

Beyond coding, Collins also specializes in graphic design and the creation of visually appealing, professional CVs. He offers a wide range of digital services aimed at helping individuals and businesses stand out, grow, and thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Driven by a belief that technology can transform lives, Collins focuses on leveraging AI and digital tools to address real-world challenges in underserved regions. His mission is to make technology accessible, spark sustainable growth, and create opportunities where they’re needed most.

Whether it’s developing websites, designing standout CVs, or implementing AI-powered solutions, Collins is committed to using technology for good. He’s always open to collaborations that drive progress and build a more connected, inclusive future.
